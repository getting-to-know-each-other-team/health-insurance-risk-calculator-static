Blood Pressure

Blood Pressure measures the force exerted on the walls of blood vessels by circulating blood. It is measured by using two numbers: Systolic (Upper number) and Diastolic (Lower number).

Keeping your blood pressure in a healthy range is important to avoid health risks such as heart disease and strokes.

Please record your blood pressure using a sphygomomanometer (blood pressure monitor), and input the Systolic and Diastolic values in the appropriate boxes below.
